ABOUT US

Yantrr Electronic Systems (Y.E.S Pvt. Ltd). is a 2010 startup by IIT Delhi (Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi) students and faculties. A very close association with academia helps us lead the innovation cycle, and thus we design products which solve current and future needs of our customers.

With core expertise in the domain of machine-to-machine (M2M) communication, and the Internet-of-everything, we serve diverse industries by adopting a platform oriented approach. We provide solutions to gather data from any industrial, medical, home and utility sensor, securely and reliably send the data to the cloud server, and run data analytics to manage and gain insight about your business and customers.

Our hardware and software are designed with an aim of ensuring interoperability, so that our customers can pick-and-choose what best suits them.
